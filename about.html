Elizabeth Bennet takes travel and landscape photographs to celebrate nature and share beauty in the imagery shown here.

I find that photography allows me to spend time outdoors whilst also exploring creatively, intuiting the essence of a place; a concept, or a process both within and without; what’s inside the frame becomes a confluence of different influences. I enjoy experiencing our vivid and changing Earth - whether that be somewhere densely populated and cultural like India, or a remote beach in Cornwall in a rare snow storm. Having a camera with me allows me to express this sense of wonder.

The photographs shown on this website have been taken over the course of the last ten years in caught moments between living and working. During this time Elizabeth studied BA (Hons) Marine and Natural History Photography and MA Art and Environment at Falmouth University, Cornwall and has since been working to fund various travels.

Most images have undergone minor post processing, such as dust spot reduction, levels and colour balance adjustments.

If you have any questions at all don’t hesitate to get in contact. Prints are available, and if you would like to use an image for anything else details on pricing can be sent on request.
